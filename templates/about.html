Our Story

MediConnect is a hackathon prototype focused on making care coordination simpler for patients and clinicians across India.

This page is a quick draft for the event and will be expanded with full details later.

Mission

Connect people to trusted care with secure, human-centered digital tools.

Focus Areas

  • Verified clinicians and safe access
  • Clear records, prescriptions, and follow-ups
  • Support for patients at every step
