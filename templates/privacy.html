How We Handle Data

This hackathon draft summarizes how we intend to protect patient and clinician information.

A full policy will be published before production use.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email, and phone
  • Profile information required for care delivery
  • Appointment notes, prescriptions, and uploaded reports
  • Basic usage analytics for product improvement

How We Use It

  • Deliver consultations, prescriptions, and follow-ups
  • Secure accounts, prevent fraud, and meet compliance needs
  • Improve the experience with aggregated insights
