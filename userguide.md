MediConnect User Guide
======================

Overview
--------
MediConnect is a telemedicine platform for patients and doctors to manage appointments, consultations, records, and follow-ups. The platform uses email-based OTP (one-time password) verification for both registration and login. Passwords are not required for patient or doctor login.

Key Roles
---------
- Patient: Book appointments, upload reports, chat with doctors, view prescriptions, and manage profile.
- Doctor: Review and accept appointments, manage schedule, add consultation notes, and issue prescriptions.
- Admin: Approve doctor accounts and manage platform data (admin login still uses password).

Access and Authentication
-------------------------
Email OTP is the only login method for patients and doctors.

Register as Patient
-------------------
1) Open the Patient Registration page.
2) Enter your profile details (name, phone, date of birth, gender, address).
3) Enter your email address.
4) Submit the form to receive a 6-digit OTP by email.
5) Enter the OTP on the verification page to complete registration.

Register as Doctor
------------------
1) Open the Doctor Registration page.
2) Enter your professional details (name, phone, license ID, specialization, clinic address, years of experience).
3) Enter your professional email address.
4) Submit the form to receive a 6-digit OTP by email.
5) Enter the OTP on the verification page to complete registration.
6) Your account remains pending until an admin approves it.

Login as Patient or Doctor (Email OTP)
--------------------------------------
1) Open the relevant login page.
2) Enter your registered email address.
3) Click "Send OTP".
4) Check your email and enter the 6-digit OTP on the verification page.
5) You will be signed in automatically after successful verification.

Resend OTP
----------
If you do not receive the OTP:
1) Click "Resend Code" on the OTP verification page.
2) A new OTP will be sent to your email address.

Notes
-----
- Passwords are not required for patient or doctor login.
- OTPs are sent only to the registered email address.
- Doctor login requires admin approval before access is granted.

Patient Experience
------------------

Patient Dashboard
-----------------
The patient dashboard provides a snapshot of upcoming and completed appointments, plus quick actions:
- Book Appointment: Opens the scheduling form.
- Upload Reports: Access medical records.
- Order Medicine: View prescriptions and related actions.

Book an Appointment
-------------------
1) Go to the Patient Dashboard.
2) Use the "Book Appointment" section to select:
	- Issue category
	- Doctor
	- Date and time
3) Add optional details such as symptoms, allergies, medications, conditions, vitals, and reports.
4) Submit the request.

View Appointments
-----------------
The "My Appointments" page shows:
- Pending, confirmed, and completed appointments.
- Consultation notes (if provided by the doctor).
- Actions such as cancel, chat, join call, and request follow-up.

Chat and Video Consultation
---------------------------
- Chat: Open the appointment chat to message the doctor.
- Video: Click "Join Call" for confirmed appointments to open the video session.

Medical Records and Reports
---------------------------
- Upload and view reports linked to appointments.
- Access past reports in the Medical Records page.

Prescriptions
-------------
- View prescriptions issued by doctors after consultations.
- Access details such as medication, dosage, and instructions.

Timeline
--------
- See a consolidated history of appointments, prescriptions, and follow-ups.

Profile and Settings
--------------------
- Update personal profile details.
- Manage notification preferences.

Doctor Experience
-----------------

Doctor Dashboard
----------------
The doctor dashboard shows:
- Daily appointments
- Total patient count
- Pending requests
- Quick actions to manage appointments and schedule

Appointments Management
-----------------------
On the Appointments page, doctors can:
- Accept or reject pending requests.
- Add consultation notes.
- Issue prescriptions.
- Mark appointments as completed.
- Join video consultations.

Schedule Management
-------------------
Use the Schedule page to set availability and manage time slots.

Patients and Reviews
--------------------
- View patient list and details.
- Read feedback and reviews.

Profile and Settings
--------------------
- Update doctor profile information.
- Manage notification preferences.

Admin Experience
----------------

Admin Login
-----------
- Admin login uses email and password.
- Only approved admins can access admin dashboard features.

Doctor Approval
---------------
- Review pending doctor registrations.
- Approve or reject doctor accounts.

Audit and Reporting
-------------------
- Review recent activity logs and reports.
- Manage user roles when needed.

Mobile Experience
-----------------

Mobile Navigation (Dashboards)
------------------------------
Patient and doctor dashboards include a consistent mobile bottom navigation bar for quick access.

Patient Bottom Navigation
-------------------------
- Home
- Appointments
- Book
- Records
- Profile

Doctor Bottom Navigation
------------------------
- Home
- Appointments
- Schedule
- Patients
- Profile

Landing Page Hamburger Menu
----------------------------
On mobile screens, the landing page uses a hamburger menu for navigation:
1) Tap the menu icon to open.
2) Select a section or login option.
3) Tap outside the menu or the close icon to dismiss.

Troubleshooting
---------------
- OTP not received: Check spam/junk and use "Resend Code".
- Login blocked for doctor: Account may be pending approval.
- File upload issues: Ensure file type is supported (pdf, png, jpg, jpeg, doc, docx).

Support
-------
For help, use the support email links on the website or contact your administrator.
